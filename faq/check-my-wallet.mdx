---
title: How can I check my wallet account details?
---

Account wallet details can be found after logging in to your account:  
**Wallets tab > Wallet > Details**  
or by using the following methods:

[`GET /v3/wallet`](/api-reference/endpoint/v3-wallet) or [`GET /v3/wallet/{id}`](/api-reference/endpoint/v3-wallet{id})
