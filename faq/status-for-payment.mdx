### Typical Status Flows

- <Badge color="gray">Draft</Badge> → <Badge color="blue">New</Badge> → <Badge color="yellow">Compliance</Badge> → <Badge color="blue">Process</Badge> → <Badge color="green">Completed</Badge>  
- <Badge color="blue">New</Badge> → <Badge color="orange">Canceled</Badge>  
- <Badge color="gray">Draft</Badge> → <Badge color="blue">New</Badge> → <Badge color="yellow">Compliance</Badge> → <Badge color="red">Rejected</Badge>  

> **Note:** `Draft` and `Compliance` statuses are optional.
