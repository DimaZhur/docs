---
title: How can I check all my cards?
---

1. To check all cards related to current account execute 
[`GET /v1/card`](/api-reference/endpoint/get-v1-card) 
1. To check all cards related to current account and specific wallet execute 
[`GET /v1/card`](/api-reference/endpoint/get-v1-card)
with id of the card wallet in wallet_id parameter
